Our makerspace also features a dedicated metalworking workshop, equipped with a variety of tools for shaping, cutting, drilling, and finishing metal. It is designed for basic fabrication, prototyping, and repair work, and is suitable for both beginners and experienced makers.

### Stationary Machines

- Bench grinder – for sharpening, deburring, and shaping metal
- Drill press – accurate drilling into a wide range of metals
- pipe-cutting bandsaw – for cutting pipes, aluminum profiles
- Bench vise – secure hold for cutting, filing, and assembly

### Handheld Power Tools

- Angle grinders
- Hand drills
- Rotary tools with cutting and grinding attachments
- Metal cutting saws
